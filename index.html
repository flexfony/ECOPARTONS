ABOUT US

OUR POLICIES

ECOPARTONS Private Limited is committed to enhance shareholder's value and emerge as a global professional service provider by providing qualitative consultancy & monitoring services in the field of environment, OHS & engineering including impact assessment, auditing, design & development, due diligence etc. by putting customer's need at the heart of our business while maintaining green, clean, safe & healthy working conditions in our activities.

We shall achieve the same through:
  • Timely delivery of services.
  • Improving accuracy in service deliverables & reduction in non-conforming services.
  • Prevention of injury & ill health.
  • Prevention of pollution & protection of environment through optimum resource utilization & efficient waste management.
  • Development of human resource through training.
  • Compile with current applicable legal requirements & other requirements to which organization subscribes.
  • Sustainable business growth & profit.
  • Communicate to all stake holders through appropriate media.
  • Continual improvement by reviewing of service provision and effectiveness of Quality, Environment and Occupational Health & Safety Management system.
